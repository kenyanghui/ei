什么是 Genos EI 模型？What is the Genos EI Model?

一个经过严格验证、基于证据的框架，由斯威本科技大学开发，通过可观察的职场行为来衡量情商。与其他模型不同，Genos 聚焦于人们多频繁展示 EI 行为——而非他们自认为有多大的能力。A rigorously validated, evidence-based framework developed at Swinburne University that measures emotional intelligence through observable workplace behaviors. Unlike other models, Genos focuses on how frequently people demonstrate EI behaviors — not just their perceived ability.

职场 EI 四维模型Workplace EI 4-Dimension Model

Daniel Goleman · Emotional Intelligence (1995)Daniel Goleman · 情商 (1995)
Self-AwarenessSelf-ManagementSocial AwarenessRelationship Management

📖 Core Theory📖 核心理论

  • EI > IQ情商 > 智商 — Emotional intelligence predicts workplace success and leadership more than cognitive ability— 情商比认知能力更能预测职场成功和领导力
  • 4-Dimension Framework四维框架: Self-Awareness → Self-Management → Social Awareness → Relationship Management：自我觉察 → 自我管理 → 社会觉察 → 关系管理
  • Neuroscience神经科学: Prefrontal cortex (reason) vs amygdala (emotion) — the "amygdala hijack" mechanism：前额叶皮层（理性）vs 杏仁核（情绪）— “杏仁核劫持”机制
  • Malleability可塑性: EI can be developed through deliberate practice, regardless of innate IQ：EI 可通过刻意练习来发展，不受先天 IQ 限制

🔑 Key Insight核心洞察: First become aware of your own emotions, then manage them. Only then can you truly understand others and build exceptional relationships.先觉察自己的情绪，再管理它们。只有这样才能真正理解他人，建立卓越的人际关系。

🛠 Practical Tools🛠 实践工具

  • Self-Awareness自我觉察: Daily emotion journal (trigger → body sensation → label → thought → behavior → outcome)：每日情绪日志（触发 → 身体感受 → 标注 → 想法 → 行为 → 结果）
  • Self-Management自我管理: STOP method (Stop → Take a breath → Observe → Proceed)：STOP 法（暂停 → 呼吸 → 观察 → 继续）
  • Social Awareness社会觉察: 3 Levels of Empathy — Cognitive → Emotional → Compassionate：共情三层次 — 认知 → 情感 → 慈悲
  • Relationship Management关系管理: Non-Violent Communication (Observe → Feel → Need → Request)：非暴力沟通（观察 → 感受 → 需要 → 请求）
  • EI Self-CheckEI 自查: Weekly 18-competency self-rating (1-5 scale), track progress over time：每周 18 项能力自评（1-5 分），追踪长期进步

MSCEIT 四分支能力模型MSCEIT 4-Branch Ability Model

Mayer & Salovey · Emotional Intelligence (1990) — Academic OrthodoxyMayer & Salovey · 情商 (1990) — 学术正统
PerceptionFacilitationUnderstandingManagement

📖 Core Theory📖 核心理论

  • EI = Cognitive AbilityEI = 认知能力: Emotional intelligence is "the ability to process emotional information" — not personality：情绪智能是“处理情绪信息的能力”— 不是人格特质
  • 4-Branch Model四分支模型: Perceiving (basic) → Facilitating → Understanding → Managing (advanced)：感知（基础）→ 促进 → 理解 → 管理（高级）
  • MSCEIT TestMSCEIT 测试: 141 items with objective right/wrong answers (expert consensus based)：141 题，有客观对错答案（基于专家共识）
  • Independent Intelligence独立智能: MSCEIT correlates with IQ at only r=0.2-0.3, proving EI is a distinct cognitive domain：MSCEIT 与 IQ 仅 r=0.2-0.3 相关，证明 EI 是独立的认知领域

🔑 Key Insight核心洞察: Real emotional intelligence isn't about "feeling good" — it's about accurately perceiving, deeply understanding, and wisely using emotional information.：真正的情商不是关于"感觉良好"— 而是准确感知、深度理解并智慧运用情绪信息。

🛠 Practical Tools🛠 实践工具

  • Perception Training感知训练: Daily "emotion labeling" — name precise emotions (not just "good/bad")：每日“情绪标注”— 说出精确的情绪名称（不只是“好/坏”）
  • Facilitation Practice促进练习: Before decisions, ask "How do I feel? What is this feeling telling me?"：决策前，问自己“我的感受是什么？这个感受在告诉我什么？”
  • Emotion Vocabulary情绪词汇: 6 basic emotions → 30+ precise emotion words：6 种基本情绪 → 30+ 个精确情绪词
  • Understanding理解: Emotion logic chain — Event → Feeling → Thought → Behavior：情绪逻辑链 — 事件 → 感受 → 想法 → 行为
  • Management Strategy Matrix管理策略矩阵: Situation Selection → Modification → Attention Deployment → Cognitive Change → Response Modulation：情境选择 → 调整 → 注意力部署 → 认知改变 → 反应调节
包含四分支详解 + 情绪词汇表 + 每日训练模板Includes 4-branch details + emotion vocabulary + daily training template

EQ-i 五因素特质模型EQ-i 5-Factor Trait Model

Reuven Bar-On · Handbook of Emotional Intelligence (1997)Reuven Bar-On · 情商手册 (1997)
Self-PerceptionSelf-ExpressionInterpersonalDecision MakingStress Management

📖 Core Theory📖 核心理论

  • EI = Traits × Social FunctionEI = 特质 × 社会功能: A set of emotional and social competencies that influence how we cope with demands：一组影响我们应对需求方式的情感和社交能力
  • 5 Factors, 15 Sub-Scales5 因素 15 子量表: Self-Perception(3) · Self-Expression(3) · Interpersonal(3) · Decision Making(3) · Stress Management(3)：自我认知(3) · 自我表达(3) · 人际(3) · 决策(3) · 压力管理(3)
  • EQ-i InventoryEQ-i 量表: 133-item self-report, one of the most widely used EI assessments globally：133 题自陈量表，全球使用最广泛的 EI 评估之一
  • Applications应用领域: Mental health assessment, career counseling, leadership development, clinical diagnosis support：心理健康评估、职业咨询、领导力发展、临床诊断支持

🔑 Key Insight核心洞察: Emotional intelligence IS your ability to cope with life's pressures — 5 factors, 15 competencies, one resilience profile.：情商就是你应对生活压力的能力 — 5 个因素、15 项能力、一个韧性档案。

🛠 Practical Tools🛠 实践工具

  • 5-Factor Self-Assessment五因素自评: Rate each sub-scale (1-7), identify strongest and weakest dimensions：对每个子量表评分（1-7），识别最强和最弱维度
  • Stress Management Toolkit压力管理工具包: 4-7-8 breathing → Cognitive restructuring → Social support activation：4-7-8 呼吸法 → 认知重构 → 社会支持激活
  • Decision Checklist决策检查表: Before major decisions, check all 5 factors are balanced：重大决策前，检查所有 5 个因素是否平衡
  • Interpersonal Practice人际练习: 10 minutes daily "stand in their shoes" — replay a conversation from the other's perspective：每天 10 分钟“换位思考”— 从对方角度重演一段对话
  • Reality Testing现实检验: Separate "facts" from "feelings" — write both lists and compare gaps：区分“事实”与“感受”— 列出两者并比较差距

Genos 职场 EI 六维模型Genos Workplace EI 6-Dimensions

Palmer & Stough · Swinburne University (Genos EI Technical Manual 2002/2008)Palmer & Stough · 斯威本科技大学 (Genos EI 技术手册 2002/2008)
6 Dimensions360° FeedbackBehavior FrequencyROI Evidence

📖 Core Theory📖 核心理论

  • Workplace-Focused职场聚焦: Unlike other models, Genos targets "workplace behavior" specifically：与其他模型不同，Genos 专门针对“职场行为”
  • Behavior Frequency行为频率: Measures "how often" you demonstrate EI behaviors, not "how much ability" you have：衡量你“多常”展示 EI 行为，而非“多少”能力
  • 6 Progressive Dimensions六个递进维度: Self-Awareness → Awareness of Others → Authenticity → Emotional Reasoning → Self-Management → Positive Influence：自我觉察 → 觉察他人 → 真实性 → 情绪推理 → 自我管理 → 积极影响力
  • 360° Assessment360° 评估: Self + manager/peer/direct report ratings reveal blind spots：自评 + 上级/同事/下属评分揭示盲点
  • ROI: EI training delivers 17-20% performance improvement, especially in sales：EI 培训带来 17-20% 的绩效提升，尤其在销售领域

🔑 Key Insight核心洞察: Workplace EI isn't "do you understand emotions" — it's "how often do you actually demonstrate EI behaviors at work every day?" Frequency = impact.：职场情商不是"你懂不懂情绪"— 而是"你每天在工作中多常展示 EI 行为？"频率 = 影响力。

🛠 Practical Tools🛠 实践工具

  • 6-Dimension Growth Plan六维成长计划: Set one "next week focus behavior" per dimension：每个维度设定一个“下周重点行为”
  • 360° Preparation Checklist360° 准备清单: Select 3-5 raters, clarify purpose and confidentiality：选择 3-5 名评估者，明确目的和保密性
  • Behavior Frequency Self-Rating行为频率自评: Rate key behaviors on Never/Occasionally/Sometimes/Usually/Always：按“从不/偶尔/有时/经常/总是”评估关键行为
  • Emotional Reasoning Template情绪推理模板: Fact data × Stakeholder emotions × Emotional impact × Integrated decision：事实数据 × 利益相关者情绪 × 情感影响 × 整合决策
  • Positive Influence Practice积极影响力练习: Daily specific + sincere positive feedback to one colleague：每天向一位同事给予具体且真诚的正面反馈

多元智能识别与发展Multiple Intelligences Identification & Development

Howard Gardner · Frames of Mind (1983) — EI Theory OriginHoward Gardner · 智能的结构 (1983) — EI 理论起源
8 IntelligencesIntrapersonalInterpersonalPersonalized Education

📖 Core Theory📖 核心理论

  • Beyond IQ超越 IQ: IQ tests only measure linguistic + logical abilities, missing the full spectrum of human intelligence：IQ 测试只测量语言和逻辑能力，忽略了人类智能的全谱系
  • 8 Intelligences八种智能: Linguistic · Logical-Mathematical · Musical · Spatial · Bodily-Kinesthetic · Interpersonal · Intrapersonal · Naturalistic：语言 · 逻辑-数学 · 音乐 · 空间 · 身体-动觉 · 人际 · 内省 · 自然观察
  • EI OriginEI 起源: "Intrapersonal" (self-awareness) and "Interpersonal" (understanding others) directly laid foundations for EI theory：“内省智能”（自我觉察）和“人际智能”（理解他人）直接为 EI 理论奠定基础
  • Individualized Development个性化发展: Everyone has a unique intelligence profile — education should be personalized：每个人有独特的智能组合 — 教育应当个性化

🔑 Key Insight核心洞察: The question isn't "how smart are you" — it's "HOW are you smart." Discovering your unique intelligence combination is discovering your talent.：问题不在于"你有多聪明"— 而在于"你如何聪明"。发现你独特的智能组合就是发现你的天赋。

🛠 Practical Tools🛠 实践工具

  • 8-Intelligence Radar八智能雷达: Self-rate each dimension (1-10), map your personal intelligence profile：自评每个维度（1-10），绘制个人智能图谱
  • Intrapersonal Growth内省智能成长: Daily 3 questions — What was my strongest emotion today? Source? What does it tell me?：每日三问 — 今天最强的情绪是什么？来源？它在告诉我什么？
  • Interpersonal Practice人际练习: Daily empathy — analyze one conversation from the other's "feeling × need × perspective"：每日共情 — 从对方的“感受 × 需要 × 视角”分析一段对话
  • Team Intelligence Mix团队智能组合: Identify each team member's strengths, assign tasks accordingly：识别每个团队成员的优势，相应分配任务
  • Education/ Parenting教育/育儿: Observe which activities your child engages in most deeply — that's their natural intelligence strength：观察孩子最投入的活动 — 那就是他们的天赋智能

1920 · 起源1920 · Origin
Edward ThorndikeEdward Thorndike
Columbia University哥伦比亚大学
先驱Pioneer

1920 年提出"社会智力"——首次提出人际理解与情绪洞察属于智力范畴。EI 理论最早的原型。In 1920, published "Social Intelligence" — first to propose that interpersonal understanding and emotional insight belong to the domain of intelligence. The earliest prototype of EI theory.

1985 · 术语诞生1985 · Term Birth
Wayne PayneWayne Payne
博士研究者Doctoral Researcher
先驱Pioneer

首次在 1985 年博士论文《情感研究》中提出"情感智能"术语。已知最早的 EI 概念学术记录。First to coin the term "Emotional Intelligence" in his 1985 doctoral thesis "A Study of Emotion." The earliest known academic record of the EI concept.

1983 · 理论根源1983 · Theoretical Roots
Howard GardnerHoward Gardner
Harvard University哈佛大学
奠基Foundational

提出多元智能理论（内省智能 + 人际智能），打破 IQ 单一维度。为 EI 理论提供了概念框架。Proposed Multiple Intelligences Theory (Intrapersonal + Interpersonal), breaking the unitary view of IQ. Provided the conceptual framework for EI theory.

1990 · 学术正统1990 · Academic Orthodoxy
John Mayer & Peter SaloveyJohn Mayer & Peter Salovey
Yale / University of New Hampshire耶鲁大学 / 新罕布什尔大学

1997 · 测量系统1997 · Measurement System
Reuven Bar-OnReuven Bar-On
临床心理学家Clinical Psychologist
奠基Foundational

开发 EQ-i（情商问卷），建立特质模型。广泛应用于心理健康、压力管理和临床评估。Developed the EQ-i (Emotional Quotient Inventory), establishing the Trait Model. Widely applied in mental health, stress management, and clinical assessment.

1995 · 普及推广1995 · Popularization

Focuses on workplace behavioral frequency rather than ability or trait — "EI can be developed." Founders of the Genos EI framework and this summit's honored contributors.

  • 识别情绪变化并准确理解触发因素Identify emotional changes and accurately understand triggers
  • 觉察情绪如何影响工作表现和决策Recognize how emotions affect work performance and decisions
  • 持续反思感受及其背后的心理需求Continuously reflect on feelings and underlying psychological needs
2

他人情绪觉察 Awareness of Others

觉察领域Awareness Domain

感知、理解并认可他人的感受。职场共情的核心——从"关注自己"转向"看见他人"。Perceiving, understanding, and acknowledging others' feelings. The core of workplace empathy — shifting from "self-focus" to "seeing others."

  • 主动觉察并准确解读他人的情绪信号Actively notice and accurately interpret others' emotional signals
  • 展现对他人的理解和尊重Demonstrate understanding and respect for others' situations
  • 在沟通中使用共情语言确认他人感受Use empathetic language to confirm others' feelings in communication

真诚表达 Authenticity

表达领域Expression Domain

开放、诚实、一致地表达想法和感受。言行一致、信守承诺，这是信任的基石。Open, honest, and consistent expression of thoughts and feelings. Walking the talk and keeping commitments as the foundation of trust.

  • 真诚表达想法和感受，不虚伪Express thoughts and feelings honestly without pretense
  • 信守承诺，言行始终一致Keep promises — align words with actions consistently
  • 在压力下坚持原则，不扭曲自我Uphold principles under pressure without distorting oneself
4

情绪推理 Emotional Reasoning

整合领域Thinking Domain

在决策和问题解决中整合情绪信息与事实数据——避免"纯逻辑"或"纯情绪"的极端。Integrating emotional information with factual data in decision-making and problem-solving — avoiding the extremes of "pure logic" or "pure emotion."

  • 做出重要决策时主动考虑情绪状态Actively consider emotional state when making important decisions
  • 权衡事实数据与情绪信息，进行综合判断Weigh factual data alongside emotional information for integrated judgment
  • 帮助团队平衡理性分析与情感智能Help teams balance rational analysis with emotional intelligence
5

自我管理 Self-Management

管理领域Regulation Domain

管理自己的情绪、时间和行为。在高压力下保持沉着、专注和高效。Managing one's emotions, time, and behavior. Maintaining composure, focus, and productivity in high-pressure situations.

  • 在压力下保持情绪稳定和理性判断Maintain emotional stability and rational judgment under stress
  • 有效管理时间和精力，避免情绪耗竭Effectively manage time and energy to avoid emotional exhaustion
  • 从挫折中快速恢复，保持积极工作状态Recover quickly from setbacks and maintain a positive work state
6

积极影响力 Positive Influence

领导领域Leadership Domain

通过解决问题、有效反馈和支持他人，营造积极健康的工作环境。EI 发展的最高境界。Creating a positive, healthy work environment through problem-solving, effective feedback, and supporting others. The highest level of EI development.

  • 主动识别和解决团队中的情绪与人际问题Proactively identify and resolve emotional and interpersonal issues in teams
  • 给予具体、真诚、及时的正面反馈与建设性意见Provide specific, sincere, timely positive feedback and constructive input
  • 以身作则，营造开放、包容、信任的团队文化Lead by example in creating an open, inclusive, trusting team culture
